Module: GraphQL::Tracing::CallLegacyTracers This trace class calls legacy-style tracer with payload hashes. New-style trace_with modules significantly reduce the overhead of tracing, but that advantage is lost when legacy-style tracers are also used (since the payload hashes are still constructed). Instance methods: analyze_multiplex, analyze_query, authorized, authorized_lazy, execute_field, execute_field_lazy, execute_multiplex, execute_query, execute_query_lazy, lex, parse, resolve_type, resolve_type_lazy, validate
